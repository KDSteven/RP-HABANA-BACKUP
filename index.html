About Us

Trusted Auto Repair Since 2007

Founded in 2007, R Habana Auto Repair Shop has been a trusted name in automotive repair, maintenance, and quality parts. What started as a single branch has now grown into seven (7) locations across Calamba City, serving hundreds of customers with the same commitment to excellence.

Every branch offers the same reliable services—from tire repair and oil change to advanced engine diagnostics—ensuring customers get the right care wherever they visit. With a team of over twenty skilled employees, we guarantee smooth operations, accurate service, and customer satisfaction.

At R Habana, we believe in combining skilled workmanship with trusted automotive products. Our hands-on approach and years of experience allow us to deliver fast yet precise services while keeping vehicles safe and road-ready.
